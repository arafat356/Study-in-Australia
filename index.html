Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about studying in Australia

How much does it cost to study in Australia?

Tuition fees vary by course and university, typically ranging from $20,000 to $50,000 AUD per year. Living expenses are approximately $21,000-$25,000 AUD annually. We help you find affordable options and scholarship opportunities.

What are the English language requirements?

Most universities require IELTS 6.0-7.0 overall (varies by course). We offer comprehensive English test preparation and can recommend pathway programs if you need to improve your English skills.

Can I work while studying in Australia?

Yes! Student visa holders can work up to 20 hours per week during studies and full-time during breaks. The minimum wage is $25.41/hour, helping you support your living expenses.

How long does the visa process take?

Student visa processing typically takes 4-6 weeks, but can vary. We recommend applying 3-4 months before your course starts. Our expert team ensures your application is complete and accurate to avoid delays.

What are the pathways to permanent residency?

Australia offers several PR pathways for graduates including skilled migration, employer sponsorship, and state nomination programs. We provide comprehensive migration advice to help you achieve your long-term goals.

Do you charge for your services?

Our initial consultation is completely FREE. We offer transparent pricing for our services with no hidden fees. Many of our university application services are provided at no cost as we receive commission from partner institutions.
